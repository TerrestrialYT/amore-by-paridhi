Our Story

Baking with passion, precision, and love.
Welcome to Amore By Paridhi.

The Journey

What started as a modest home kitchen experiment quickly blossomed into a beloved bespoke bakery here in Raipur. At Amore By Paridhi, we believe that desserts are more than just an end to a meal—they are a centerpiece of celebration, a gesture of affection, and a moment of pure joy.

Our commitment is simple: creating luxurious, 100% eggless baked goods that compromise on nothing. Every cake, dessert jar, and pastry that leaves our kitchen is hand-crafted from scratch using the finest ingredients we can source.

100% Eggless & FSSAI Certified

We understand the importance of dietary choices and cultural preferences. That's why our entire facility is strictly eggless. We take pride in our FSSAI certification, ensuring that every bite you take meets the highest standards of hygiene and quality.
